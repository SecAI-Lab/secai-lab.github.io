Welcome to the SecAI Lab


SecAI is a security research group in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at the College of Computing and Informatics, Sungkyunkwan University. As computing devices (e.g., laptops, workstations, mobile, and IoT devices) and services (e.g., cloud platforms, large language models) continue to grow rapidly, securiy and privacy have become essential at all times. Accordingly, our lab focuses on practical software security and emerging technologies that require robust security foundations. In particular, we conduct research on AI-assisted security and securing AI models, from which our lab name originated. Our research interests span a broad range of topics where deep learning can be applied, including, but not limited to, executable binary analysis (e.g., benign software and malware), code semantics, advanced attack detection and prevention, digital forensics, etc.

Since 2021, the lab has been steadily growing with active research underway. If you think you are someone who a) enjoys intellectual exploration; b) desires to uncover the unknown digital world; or c) works collaboratively toward making the world more secure, we encourage you to apply by completing the application form. After a careful review, we will contact condidates directly. Please note that applications via email may not be considered. Applicants should be motivated by a genuine interest in research. Applications driven primarily by a) degree completion, b) employment postponement, or c) financial reasons are unlikely to be a good fit for the lab.
