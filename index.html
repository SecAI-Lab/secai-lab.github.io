Welcome to the SecAI Lab


We are a security research group in the Department of Computer Science and Engineering at the College of Computing and Informatics, Sungkyunkwan University. The number of computing devices, such as laptops, workstations, mobile devices, Internet of Things, and cloud services, has been increasing rapidly and is increasingly communicating with each other. Accordingly, a vast amount of digitized data becomes available, necessitating information security across the board.

The SecAI lab focuses on system/software security and emerging technologies that require security. In particular, we are working on security with artificial intelligence (AI), where our lab name originates from. Our interests span a range of topics where deep learning can be applied, including, but not limited to, executable binary analysis (e.g., benign software and malware), code semantics, advanced attack detection and prevention, and digital forensics.
